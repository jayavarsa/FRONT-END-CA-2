Game Controls

  • Control your paddle with the mouse cursor
  • Move the paddle top and bottom to hit the ball

Game Rules

  • The game starts with the ball in the center
  • You and the computer take turns hitting the ball
  • Each player can score a point by making the opponent miss
  • The first player to reach 11 points wins the game
